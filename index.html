About Me

I describe myself as a researcher specializing in the digital transformation of small and medium-sized enterprises. I am eager to translate my research findings into practical solutions that can directly help businesses enhance their competitiveness and achieve sustainable development.

Research interests: Digitalization and Information Management, Knowledge Management and IT Strategy.

Research

My research focuses on how internal R&D and external collaborations influence firm innovation performance.
